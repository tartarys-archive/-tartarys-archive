# tartarys-archive
This account exists to preserve ideas, texts, and standards
that are placed under community witnessing rather than authority.

No ownership is claimed here.
No enforcement is exercised.
Only authorship, intent, and chronology are recorded.

What matters is not who controls these ideas,
but that they cannot be silently erased or rewritten.

- Not a company
- Not a DAO

# How this archive is used
Documents published here may be:
- referenced publicly
- forked or mirrored
- cited as prior art or public disclosure
- discussed, criticized, or ignored

They may not be silently altered in place.
History remains readable.
